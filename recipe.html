How to make: Ham and Swiss Omlete

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Steps Needs to follow

  1. In a small nonstick skillet, melt butter over medium heat
  2. Whisk eggs
  3. add egg mixture to skillet
  4. add ham and seasoning
  5. flip if you can
  6. enjoy
